SpeakerQueue

Instructions:

SpeakerQueue allows you to easily manage a rotation of speakers in your Zoom meeting. Simply type the names of your participants into the textbox to begin. Your list of participants will appear below the textbox. Use the Remove button to remove a participant and the Done reading button to move on to the next participant. To add more participants, simply enter more names in the textbox. Don't worry about capitalization, I've taken care of it for you! This site was created by Nabil Baugher in one night. Enjoy!

Type names separated by a comma, then press enter.
